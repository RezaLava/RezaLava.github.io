About me

Hi, I’m Reza. I was that quiet kid sitting at the corner politely; that's until a friend or cousin showed up. I loved reading about dinosaurs in encyclopedias for kids.


I tried a handful of fields before settling in design. I didn't like civil engineering, what I majored in, it just wasn't for me. I spare you the details of how I got into teaching English, animation, graphic design, 3D design and finally, product design.

All my previous experiences help me making great products in a unique way.

I have been designing for over 3 years now.
